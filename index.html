Channel 01 · About the project

Urban living, reimagined for Dubai's professionals

City Tower brings a calmer pace to urban living on Sheikh Zayed Road. The 93-storey address offers 695 residences and hospitality-driven services designed for fast-paced professional lifestyles.

Situated between DIFC, Downtown, and Jumeirah — Dubai's most connected corridor — City Tower gives relocating professionals the rare combination of prime location and uncompromised living standards. Layouts are larger than typical Dubai apartments, with intuitive spaces that support work, balance, and long-term comfort.

  • 5-minute walk to Emirates Towers Metro Station
  • Quick links to DIFC and Downtown Dubai
  • 15 minutes to Dubai International Airport (DXB)
  • Direct access to Sheikh Zayed Road and Jumeirah Garden City
  • Developed by H&H — leasing partner betterhomes
